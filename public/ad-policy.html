Ad Policy

Last updated: January 2026 — Our commitment to transparent, responsible advertising.

📢 BookMyCrypto maintains strict editorial independence. Our market data, news coverage and rankings are never influenced by advertisers. Paid placements are always clearly labelled.

Our Advertising Philosophy

At BookMyCrypto, we believe advertising should enhance — not disrupt — your experience. We are selective about the partners we work with and the types of ads we display. Our platform will never show misleading, deceptive, or predatory advertisements.

We are committed to maintaining the trust of our users. If you ever see an ad that concerns you, please report it to dhiren@bookmycrypto.com.

What We Accept

  • Regulated cryptocurrency exchanges and trading platforms
  • Established blockchain projects with verifiable track records
  • Crypto wallets and security products
  • DeFi protocols with audited smart contracts
  • Crypto education platforms and courses
  • NFT marketplaces and Web3 infrastructure
  • Financial services licensed in their jurisdiction

What We Do Not Accept Strictly Prohibited

  • Unregistered or unlicensed financial products promising guaranteed returns
  • Pump-and-dump schemes or market manipulation services
  • Scam tokens, rug-pull projects, or fraudulent ICOs
  • Misleading claims about investment performance
  • Content targeting minors
  • Malware, phishing sites, or any harmful software
  • Anonymous projects with no verifiable team or audit
  • Gambling services without proper licensing

Disclosure & Transparency

All paid advertisements and sponsored content on BookMyCrypto are clearly identified with one of the following labels:

  • "Sponsored" — for paid listing placements
  • "Advertisement" — for display banner ads
  • "Sponsored Content" — for editorial-style paid articles
  • "Partner" — for long-term strategic partnerships

Our editorial team operates independently of our advertising team. Advertisers have no influence over our market rankings, news selection, or data presentation.

Ad Review Process

All advertisements submitted to BookMyCrypto go through a manual review process before going live. We verify the legitimacy of the advertiser, review all creative assets for compliance, and check that landing pages match the ad content. We reserve the right to reject any advertisement at our sole discretion without explanation.

Approved ads are monitored continuously. Ads that are found to violate our policy after approval will be removed immediately and the advertiser may be permanently banned from our platform.

User Controls

We respect your experience. If you encounter an advertisement that you believe violates our ad policy — including ads that are deceptive, offensive, or inappropriate — please report it immediately to dhiren@bookmycrypto.com with a screenshot and the URL. We investigate all reports within 48 hours.
