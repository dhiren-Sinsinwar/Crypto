Who We Are

BookMyCrypto is a free, real-time cryptocurrency tracking platform built for both beginners and experienced traders. We believe everyone deserves access to professional-grade market data without paying a cent.

Our platform aggregates live pricing data, breaking crypto news, and market intelligence — all in one clean, fast interface inspired by the world's best financial platforms.

What We Offer

Live Prices: Real-time price feeds powered by Binance WebSocket — the same data source used by professional trading desks.

Market Data: Track market cap, 24h volume, price changes across 1h, 24h and 7d timeframes for the top 20 cryptocurrencies.

News Feed: Curated crypto news from leading sources including CoinDesk and CryptoCompare, updated every 5 minutes.

Coin Detail Pages: Deep-dive into any coin with interactive price charts, supply stats, ATH/ATL data and useful links.

Our Data Sources

We use industry-standard, trusted APIs to power our platform: CoinGecko for comprehensive market data, Binance for real-time WebSocket price streams, Alternative.me for the Fear & Greed Index, and CryptoCompare and CoinDesk for news.
